About

Hi my name is Alex Diaz and I graduated with a bachelor degree in mechanical engineering from UC San Diego in 2025 where I worked heavily with the engineering organization Triton Solar Car to build a solar-powered EV for competition. On the team, I served as Business Lead for one year then Project Lead for two years. Through my time on the team, I grew to enjoy working collaboratively, and to put my efforts towards work that is motivated by mutual interest in technology that creates an impact. Through my time at school, I also worked on a number of other projects to develop hands-on engineering experience to supplement my formal education. Such projects include a regenerative braking trailer, a pH measurement testbed, and several more.
I am interested in pursuing a career in the research and development of new technologies, either through the development of research instrumentation, or the development of existing technologies in a human-centered field such as biotech or sustainable technologies.
