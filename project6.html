Robot Arm Team Competition

This team project focused on creating a robotic arm with a limited budget to compete against other teams in the same class. In this contest, each team was to design, test, analyze, fabricate, and deploy their robot to take parts from the various locations in the arena and place them back in certain target areas within the arena.
