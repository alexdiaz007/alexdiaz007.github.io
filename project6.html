Robot Arm Team Competition

This team project focused on creating a robotic arm with a set budget and limited number of motors to compete against other teams in the same class. In this contest, each team was to design, test, analyze, fabricate, and deploy their robot to take parts from the various locations in the arena and place them back in certain target areas within the arena.

skills applied

This project was for my MAE 3 class during my second year in university where I worked with a team of two other students to create a robot to compete against other teams within our class. Together, we worked to brainstorm strategies and ideas before then moving into dividing up the work. My main contribution for this project was the design and fabrication of the jointed arm piece that allows the robot to be able to point its claw either straight out or down to the ground without the use of an additional motor. Additionally, I also helped to determine high priority tasks for the team and delegate those tasks.
