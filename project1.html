Triton Solar Car

Founded in 2015, the Triton Solar Car at UC San Diego is a dynamic, student-led project dedicated to designing, building, testing, and racing solar-powered electric vehicles. We bring together passionate students from diverse academic backgrounds, ranging from engineering to business, to push the boundaries of sustainable transportation. This team plans to compete in the Formula Sun Grand Prix before then moving onto the American Solar Challenge.

skills applied

I served on the Triton Solar Car team through the entirety of my undergraduate education, starting out on the Business team wiring grant applications and creating promotional graphics and renders for the team. My second year, I became Business Lead where I worked to foster better promotion for the team on campus, and throughout the San Diego area. It was then that I started to manage a small team of my own to be able to fulfill the needs of the team from the business side. For my third and fourth years, I acted as Project Lead of the team, overseeing all aspects of the project, managing a team of over 20 members, and being the main point of contact with our advisors, professors, and business partners. At the same time, I also worked directly on the design of our current chassis and performed the necessary impact analysis required by the race regulations.
